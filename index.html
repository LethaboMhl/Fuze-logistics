ABOUT COMPANY

Modern Mining & Logistics Solutions

FUZE HOLDINGS & LOGISTICS (PTY) LTD is a 100% black-owned, youth-led logistics and transport company specializing in bulk commodity transportation, mining logistics and fleet-based operational support across South Africa.

Founded by entrepreneur Itumeleng Dylan Ngcobo, the company was established to build a scalable, disciplined and high-performance logistics brand capable of supporting mining houses, industrial operators and supply chain partners.

The business focuses on side tipper transportation, operational discipline, route optimization, fuel management systems and technology-driven fleet management.
